Binary

Binary is often reffered to as base 2, and consists of the digits 0 and 1. It represents either a present electrical signal or a signal not present.

Decimal

The decimal number system is the number system most commonly used. It is composed of the digits 0-9. To the left shows conversions between multiple number systems.

Hexadecimal

The hexadecimal number system is based on 16 digits, 0-9 and A-F. This is useful in both picking RGB colors and for representing bytes, as it can easily reduce into a round hexidecimal number.
